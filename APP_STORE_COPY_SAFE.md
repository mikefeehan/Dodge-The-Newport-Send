## Description
Dodge Mike's Newport Send is a fast neon arcade game inspired by Newport Beach.

Play as Mike across 10 themed districts. Each level gives you 3 lives and 30 seconds to clear the timer. When you advance, your score carries forward and your lives reset for the next level.

Collect power-ups, build near-miss combos, and try to finish the full 10-level run with the highest score possible.

Features:

• 10 Newport-inspired districts
• 3 lives per level
• Score carries across the full run
• Fast arcade movement
• Power-ups including Slow, Shield, VIP Clear, 2x Score, and Extra Life
• Near-miss combo scoring
• Local Top-5 leaderboard
• Neon synthwave visuals

Simple to learn. Hard to master. Built for quick runs, big scores, and maximum Send energy.
