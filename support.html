Support

Need help with Dodge Mike's Newport Send?

Email: support@bet2sweat.com

When you write in, please include

  • Device type (e.g., iPhone 15, Pixel 8, desktop)
  • Browser or app version
  • What happened
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • A screenshot or screen recording if available

Common fixes

  • Press Play before expecting music — browsers require a tap before audio can start.
  • On desktop, steer with the arrow keys / WASD or just move the mouse.
  • On mobile, drag anywhere on the screen to steer — Mike follows your finger, so your thumb doesn't need to cover him.
  • Press P to pause and M to mute at any time.
  • If saved scores look wrong, clear your browser or app storage and restart the game.
