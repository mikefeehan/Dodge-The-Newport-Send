# App Store Asset Guide

## Final icon

Use the final square icon with:

- Mike centered as the main character
- Dark synthwave Newport background
- Electric cyan and magenta glow
- Readable title treatment
- High contrast at small sizes

Required export:

- `icon.png` — 1024 x 1024 PNG

Recommended additional exports:

- `icons/icon-512.png` — 512 x 512 PNG
- `icons/icon-192.png` — 192 x 192 PNG
- `icons/apple-touch-icon.png` — 180 x 180 PNG
- `icons/favicon-32.png` — 32 x 32 PNG
- `icons/icon-maskable-512.png` — 512 x 512 PNG with safe padding for Android maskable icons

## Character art

Use the final Mike character with:

- Blue eyes
- Black hat
- Black tee shirt
- Black pants
- Clean transparent background
- Strong neon edge readability

Required export:

- `player.png` — transparent PNG used by the game

## App Store screenshots

Recommended iPhone screenshot story:

1. Title / start screen
   Caption: `Survive the Send`

2. Early gameplay
   Caption: `Dodge the Newport signs`

3. Power-up moment
   Caption: `Grab power-ups and stay alive`

4. Combo moment
   Caption: `Chain near-misses for big points`

5. Game over / leaderboard
   Caption: `Chase the local high score`

## Screenshot sizing

Use App Store Connect's required screenshot sizes for the devices selected at submission. At minimum, prepare large iPhone screenshots and crop from clean gameplay captures.

Keep screenshots high contrast, readable, and uncluttered. Use the real final icon and final player character in all marketing assets.

## App Store visual positioning

The game should feel:

- Neon
- Fast
- Local
- Funny
- Arcade
- Newport-inspired
- Easy to understand in one screenshot

Avoid making screenshots too dark in the center. The player character and falling signs need to read clearly on mobile.
