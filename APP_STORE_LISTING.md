## Full description
Dodge Mike's Newport Send is a fast, neon arcade dodging game inspired by Newport Beach nightlife.

You play as Mike, weaving through falling glowing bar-sign blocks across 10 themed districts. Survive each 30-second level, carry your score forward, grab power-ups, and chain near-miss combos to climb the local leaderboard.

Features:

• 10 Newport-inspired levels with rising difficulty
• Fast arcade dodging gameplay
• Power-ups including Slow, Shield, VIP Clear, 2x Score, and Extra Life
• Near-miss combo scoring for big points
• Desktop and mobile-friendly controls
• Local Top-5 leaderboard
• Synthwave neon visuals and party arcade energy

Simple to learn. Hard to survive. Built for quick runs, high scores, and absolute Send energy.
